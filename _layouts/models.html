OG25

Our smallest model, the OG25 aerobically processes an average of 24lbs of food waste per hour, depending on the food waste composition. It’s a heavy-duty machine with stainless steel construction, which weighs 800lbs. Food waste can be added regularly to achieve maximum digestion capacity. The OG25 utilizes ORCA EcoWaste Digester Bio Chips to hold high levels of Microorganisms that digest organic waste.

  • • Weighs 800lbs
  • • 4'2" x 2'11" x 4'1"
  • • Can process 24lbs of food waste per hour
  • • Can divert up to 95 tons of food waste per year from landfill
