ORCA EcoWaste Digester.

ORCA EcoWaste Digester is the ultimate waste management system. Working on site in your facility to “digest” all your food waste, ORCA EcoWaste Digester turns it into environmentally safe water that flows straight into the sewage system. With no bags or garbage bins of food waste to dispose of, it ends the need for food waste to go to landfill.

So how does it work?

The ORCA EcoWaste Digester is a machine that “digests” up to 1 ton of food waste per day. Within 24 hours, this technology turns food waste into environmentally safe water that flows straight into the sewage system.

The sustainable technology is housed in a stainless steel container, with a door on the top which employees open to throw food waste inside. Within the container, the ORCA EcoWaste Digester’s proprietary, natural Microorganism solution works with water and recycled plastic Bio Chips to quickly break down the waste. Through a process called “aerobic digestion,” the ORCA EcoWaste Digester technology constantly churns all this material, adding oxygen that accelerates the time it takes to transform the food waste disposal into earth-friendly water.

ORCA EcoWaste Digester versus landfill.

For every 2 tons of food waste treated each week by ORCA EcoWaste Digester not ending up in landfill, approximately 3.2 tons of landfill gas emissions are prevented. This saving of emissions is equivalent to the operation of one car for 6 months. And around 500 litres of Leachate, the liquid that drains from landfill, is saved from our water tables and waterways.

With American's generating around 254 million tons of trash annually, contributing to around 90% of the non-hazardous landfills within the US, even a fraction of that waste being processed by ORCA EcoWaste Digester will achieve a signifiant improvement to our environment.
